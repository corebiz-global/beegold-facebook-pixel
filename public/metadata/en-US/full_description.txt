## About

This application allows you to connect your store to the Facebook ad services.
You will be able to create ads and manage campaigns in real time through a Facebook "Ads Manager" account.

## Functionalities

- Create ads that will appear on Facebook applications and services, including Instagram;
- Edit ads, target audience and schedules;
- View the performance of your ads in real time.

## How to set up

1. Install the app from VTEX App Store;
2. Add your Facebook Pixel ID. You can get this value from the [Ads Manager Account](https://www.facebook.com/ads/manager);
3. Save your changes.

## Comments

- For the management of ads, you must have an "Ads Manager" Facebook account.
